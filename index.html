Introduction

Full-stack developer with an eye for design.

Hello, my name is Alyssa and I am a software developer and dog lover. Above is a picture of my new puppy, Ranger. I just recently graduated from Purdue University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. It was there that I gained the majority of my experience in programming. I am interested in most areas of computer science including AI, graphics, embedded systems, security, and HCI. However, at the moment I am most interested in web development.

I consider myself a full-stack developer because I have worked extensively with both the front-end and the back-end but I feel a greater passion for the front-end. I enjoy the front-end more because it is the way the user sees the product. I believe a good back-end is crucial to the success of a webpage, but without an intuitive, responsive design the user will not be interested in the product to begin with. It is my goal to make sure the entire product is appreciated for what it is through an interface the user can admire and understand.
